System Details

The FKS Trading Platform is a containerized application running in Docker. The platform provides comprehensive tools for backtesting and optimizing gold trading strategies with high performance and reliability.


Built with Python, FastAPI, NiceGUI, and modern data science libraries, the platform offers a seamless experience for traders and analysts working with gold market data.
